HOW MUCH DOES A GOLDSAINTE FRANCHISE COST?

When considering the cost of opening a franchise, it is important to acknowledge that starting any small business entails a commitment of time, effort, and financial resources. This holds true for investing in a Goldsainte franchise opportunity as well.

 

But when you ask — how much does it cost to open a franchise? — you can’t answer that question without considering the benefits of a well-established and proven business model that provides guidance, continuous training and support, access to shared resources, and various other benefits.

Franchise Development Options
Goldsainte offers two franchise options for individuals who are interested in establishing and operating a luxury ride share franchise.

Goldsainte XL

 

The first option is Goldsainte XL, which requires an estimated average investment ranging from $177,500 to $320,000.

 

  • Franchisee leases or purchases five (5) Infiniti QX80's
  • Minimum net worth of $400,000
  • Minimum liquid assets of $125,000
  • Approximately 8-12 weeks to open

Goldsainte Premium

 

The second option is Goldsainte Premium, available in select US cities. This option requires an estimated average investment ranging from $240,000 to $394,000.

 

  • Franchisee leases or purchases two Bentley, two Mercedes Maybach and one customized Mercedes Sprinter van
  • Minimum net worth of $1,000,000
  • Minimum liquid assets of $300,000
  • Approximately 3-6 months to open
Please note that the specific investment range may vary depending on factors such as location, area, market conditions, and the vehicle resale market.
